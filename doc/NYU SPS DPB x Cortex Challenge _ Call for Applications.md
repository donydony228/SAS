# **Structure and Experience** {#structure-and-experience}

This opportunity is open to a limited number of students. Selected participants will form teams to compete in the NYU SPS DPB x Cortex Challenge, a week-long, hands-on analytics competition.

The challenge kicks off with a **mandatory training session** where participants will be guided through the Cortex analytics simulation game and learn how to navigate the SAS Viya for Learners software platform.

After training, each team will build and refine their predictive models, and spend the following week competing in the game. At the end of the competition, teams will present their final solution to a panel of experts from NYU SPS, SAS, and HEC Mont[réal](https://www.hec.ca/en/) for feedback. The results will be posted on a leaderboard, and the top team will receive special recognition.

# **Challenge Focus: Fundraising Scenario** {#challenge-focus:-fundraising-scenario}

All NYU teams will take on a [fundraising campaign simulation](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1yntaQNncHgzDZcWhmdA9mWgMUsty5G-2/view?usp=sharing). Your goal is to help a nonprofit organization raise as much money as possible using predictive modeling tools on SAS Viya for Learners.

You’ll use real-world data to determine which potential donors to contact for the greatest impact, balancing outreach costs with the potential return. Contact comes with a price, so strategy is key\!

We’ll provide the background on the organization, a dataset of potential donors, and access to SAS® software. Once your team finalizes its donor strategy, you’ll upload your contact list to the leaderboard and see how your results compare with other teams.

# **What You’ll Gain** {#what-you’ll-gain}

Through this experience, participants will learn to thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative environment that encourages creativity and innovation. You’ll apply data-driven problem-solving to real business challenges with guidance from SAS experts.

The Cortex Challenge is a unique opportunity to build hands-on experience in data science, business analytics, and predictive modeling while sharpening your teamwork, strategic thinking, and presentation skills under time constraints.

# **Application Process** {#application-process}

* Students must apply in **teams of three to five members** using this designated [**Google Form**](https://forms.gle/BzXZFsyhTKBUKgb97).   
* Each application must include responses to the questions below. Each response should be 200 words or fewer.

You may include examples of your experience in areas such as data literacy, data analysis, use of analytics tools, basic statistics, problem-solving under pressure, or your team’s overall growth mindset and approach to learning.
